# Product Strategy Roadmap

## Why We Changed Direction

The product is no longer trying to be a generic market dashboard.
It is becoming an `AI research operating system` for busy stock investors who need:

- a short daily market brief
- deep ticker and sector pages
- actionable buy / wait / avoid guidance
- cross-market opportunity mapping across US and Korea
- an always-on internal AI product team that keeps improving the product

## What We Borrowed From The Market

### StockNow

- fast event monitoring
- earnings / calendar / briefing orientation
- language and time-zone reduction for US market information

### The Rich

- portfolio and watchlist gravity
- social proof around what to watch
- simulation and risk framing

### BestWay

- simplified explanation for difficult market information
- challenge / routine loop that keeps users coming back
- lower barrier for beginner-to-intermediate investors

### Moneytoring

- daily personalized briefing
- only the key issues, not endless news
- AI-organized routine around favorite assets and keywords

## Product Principle

The winning experience is not “more data”.
It is `faster conviction`.

That means every major surface should follow this order:

1. What changed
2. Why it matters
3. What to do now
4. What related names move next

## What We Added In This Phase

- ticker deep-dive pages
- sector deep-dive pages
- ticker / sector search with CTA into deep analysis
- cross-market related opportunity cards
- favorite-to-home flow
- EV mobility and battery chain themes
- catalyst watch blocks on ticker and sector pages
- bull / base / bear scenario cards for ticker pages
- morning routine block for sector pages

## Gap Between Current Product And Best-In-Class Products

### Today

- good insight framing
- real data-backed charts and ticker analysis
- cross-market structure exists
- AI pipeline and agent meeting infrastructure exists

### Still Missing

- portfolio-native experience
- truly dense but clean “watch everything at once” home
- stronger event / catalyst calendar
- richer related-company graph beyond same-sector mapping
- more accurate theme-specific relevance ranking for news
- better retention loop through routines, alerts, and recurring briefs

## Detailed Roadmap

### Stage 1. Insight-First MVP

Goal:
Turn the product from a research feed into an insight product.

Scope:

- keep `news / ticker / agent meeting` core tabs
- make search lead to deep ticker and sector pages
- show favorite tickers back on home
- ensure `news -> analysis -> action` structure everywhere

Status:
Mostly done

### Stage 2. Conviction Engine

Goal:
Help users decide whether to buy, wait, or avoid.

Scope:

- catalyst timeline by ticker and sector
- bull / base / bear scenario engine
- sector-to-beneficiary opportunity map
- stronger technical pattern ranking
- better related-news clustering by theme

Status:
In progress

### Stage 3. Personal Routine Layer

Goal:
Make users open the product every morning because it saves time.

Scope:

- personalized morning briefing
- favorites-based home layout
- routine cards: `today`, `this week`, `what changed`
- alert center for favorite tickers and sectors
- saved stacks for themes like AI, semis, EV, oil

Status:
Partially started

### Stage 4. Portfolio Gravity

Goal:
Make the product care about what the user owns, not just what the market says.

Scope:

- portfolio import or manual holding input
- exposure summary by sector and theme
- portfolio-level risk / catalyst view
- “why your portfolio moved” recap
- “hidden correlated positions” analysis

Status:
Not started

### Stage 5. Network Graph Intelligence

Goal:
Move from sector lists to real relationship intelligence.

Scope:

- customer / supplier / material / competitor graph
- US-KR spillover mapping
- beneficiary / laggard / hedge relationship cards
- graph-based related opportunities

Status:
Not started

### Stage 6. AI Operating Team

Goal:
Run PM / Trader / QA / CTO / DA style product improvement continuously.

Scope:

- hourly or scheduled AI team review
- issue creation from product gaps
- quality scoring for briefs and analysis
- idea ranking by effort vs impact
- implementation suggestions tied to code areas

Status:
Partially done

### Stage 7. Distribution And Retention

Goal:
Turn the product into a habit and a trusted companion.

Scope:

- real newsletter delivery
- Telegram / email / push channels
- recurring weekly “theme memo”
- learning content for difficult concepts
- social proof or shared thesis pages

Status:
Early

## Success Metrics

### Product Metrics

- % of users who go from headline to ticker deep dive
- % of users who favorite at least one ticker or sector
- repeat open rate for daily briefing
- CTA expansion rate on deep pages
- share of sessions reaching `buy / wait / avoid` surfaces

### Quality Metrics

- false-positive rate on related opportunities
- relevance score of sector headline clustering
- analysis usefulness rating
- agent idea to shipped feature conversion rate

## CEO / PO Call

The product should not try to beat every finance app at everything.
It should beat them at one sharp job:

`help me understand what matters and what I should do next in under 3 minutes, then let me go deeper only when I want to.`

If we keep that bar, the product can become competitive very quickly because the existing market is still fragmented:

- one app is good at briefs
- another is good at portfolio gravity
- another is good at community
- another is good at beginner explanation

Our opportunity is to connect all four inside one coherent research flow.
